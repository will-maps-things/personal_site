About Me

Hi! Thanks for stopping by.

I’m a DC-based research analyst interested in human rights, livable cities, and democratized access to reliable information. I love to create compelling multimedia research narratives with text and visuals, especially data-rich maps. Most recently, I served as a Senior Research Analyst at Strategy Risks, where I conducted due diligence on human rights and political risk in supply chains abroad.

I’m currently seeking entry- or mid-level research roles in a variety of subject areas, including but not limited to U.S.-China policy, clean transportation, and information manipulation. If you’re reading this, drop me an email so we can make a real connection!

I hold a BA in Global Affairs and an Advanced Certificate in Mandarin Chinese from Yale University. Click Portfolio to browse examples of my academic work.
